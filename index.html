Hi there!

I'm Creative Karolina. A female MacGyver, but creative :) I've always been interested in daydreaming along with expressing myself through creativity in the broadest sense.

I believe that where there's a will, there's a way, and it's a waste of time to engage in boring activities or stay still like a pole. One must fulfill oneself, do what they love, and then efficiency will soar. I've traveled a lot in my life, both near and far.
I've tried to communicate in many languages. After all these encounters and experiences, one thing I know for sure.
The language of love, kindness, and understanding is universal. Things that seem impossible to me don't exist; only the time of realization may extend.
I crossed three countries in three days hitchhiking and managed to accomplish what seemed to be a mission impossible. The Balkan Express.
It's time to present to the world my fearless toughness with delicate sensitivity to create beautiful things.

Clickissimo came to me in a dream at night, and I knew I wanted to follow this romantically Italian path. I'll prepare the best photo sessions for you in the farthest corners of the globe. Of course, for near ones too :) And at the same time, you and I will support local photographers and the community that will be featured in your photos.

It will be clicked out!

Clickissimo is a place where beautiful things happen.
We provide photo sessions for your products along with social media coverage for your company abroad. We are responsible for the concept, styling, and selection of carefully curated locations for the photo shoots. Preferences and project details are discussed individually before commencing work.
We are a friend who supports local creators, artists, and communities. Participants in the project are individuals from the countries covered by the project, unless otherwise agreed upon in advance.
