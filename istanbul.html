Explore Istanbul's Locations!

Roof Top

An undeniable attraction of Istanbul. As the metropolis is situated on many hillsides just above the shores of the Bosphorus, the view from above is stunning, breathtaking. Pigeons, the vast river, extraordinary minaret towers make the photos mesmerizing. There's a lot happening here. Patterns, colors, countless tiny buildings from a perspective, all seasoned with bold oriental sensuality. The place showcases the majesty of a city where 16 million people live.

Balat

Everything colorful, dynamic, full of life is right there. A vibrant district with countless pastel or neon facades, colorful stairs, hanging pots, or umbrellas. It's cheerful and bustling :) There's no boredom or seriousness. The energy resonates in very positive vibrations.

Ortakoy

The surroundings of an incredibly charming mosque right on the riverbanks with a majestic bridge connecting Europe to Asia in the background. This is the location where all the hits from Turkish TV series echo in your head. This is the location where one falls in love with this giant city with a soul and bows, filled with culture. We don't walk past it indifferently and get lost in its majesty, yet delicacy. For those seeking delicate and sensual scenery.
